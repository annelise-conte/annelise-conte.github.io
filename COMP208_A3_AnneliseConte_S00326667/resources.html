Resources

This page showcases TWO teaching and learning resources that support both students and educators in the area of Technology and Applied Studies (TAS). It includes an interactive digital tool, such as the ACU Grade Converter and a key curriculum material like the Stage 6 Food Technology Syllabus

ACU Grade Converter

This interactive JavaScript tool allows you to convert a numerical mark (0–100) for an assignment into its corresponding ACU letter grade. The Reset button clears the input if you make a mistake.

Stage 6 Food Technology Syllabus

Download the Stage 6 Food Technology Syllabus to analyse key NESA requirements for the course and learning outcomes.
